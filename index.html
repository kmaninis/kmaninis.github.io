Kevis-Kokitsi Maninis

Computer Vision Lab
ETH Zürich, Switzerland

Hello! I am Kevis. I finished my PhD at the Computer Vision Lab of ETH Zürich, supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool. I received my M.Eng degree from National Technical University of Athens, School of Electrical and Computer Engineering. Before joining CVL, I was a member of the CVSP lab of N.T.U.A, where I worked on Human Action Recognition in videos with Prof. Petros Maragos.
I have interned in Facebook AI Research (FAIR), where I worked on multi-task learning with Prof. Iasonas Kokkinos. My main research interest is deep learning for dense prediction tasks.

Check out our group's segmentation projects here.

News

Oct 2019: I finished my PhD and I am currently a Research Scientist at Google.

Aug 2019: We released the code for Attentive Single-Tasking of Multiple Tasks (ASTMT).

Feb 2019: Our paper on multi-task learning was accepted in CVPR 2019. Work done at FAIR with Ilija Radosavovic and Iasonas Kokkinos.

May 2018: Journal extension of OSVOS accepted by T-PAMI.

Mar 2018: Deep EXTReme cut (DEXTR) was accepted in CVPR 2018! PyTorch code, pre-computed results, and models available!!

Jan 2018: Our work on 3D from CNN keypoints was accepted by Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L)/ICRA! ~100μm accuracy for stereo reconstruction in retinal surgery!!
